Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 1-2 pounds
spaghetti 1 box
salt little bit
water 1 pot
Marinara 1 jar

Step 1: Bring water to boil and then break a meatinto small pieces and cook it for 10-15 minutes

Step 2: Take marinara sauce and mix it with the meat

Step 3: Cook spaghetti for the amount of time that said on the box (Typically 7-11 minutes)

Step 4: Serve your spaghetti with sauce and you can also use cheese on top
